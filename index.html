About me

Since January 2022, I am a postdoctoral researcher working with Lorenzo Rosasco at Università di Genova, in Italy.

I hold a PhD in Statistics from CREST (Center for Research in Economics and Statistics) and Institut Polytechnique de Paris. My supervisors were Arnak Dalalyan and Victor-Emmanuel Brunel. My manuscript is available here.

I am mainly interested in statistical learning theory, high-dimensional statistics and fair learning.

I used to co-organize the Stat·Eco·ML bi-monthly seminar at CREST.
